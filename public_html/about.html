About CoreFlux

A unified platform built for modern professional services—people, finance, and operations in one place.

Our Story

CoreFlux started with a simple idea: make it radically easier for firms to run their core—people, finance, accounting, tax, reporting, and CRM—without juggling five different tools. We’re building a platform that scales from solo practices to multi-entity groups with robust multi-tenant controls, custom fields, and streamlined approvals.

Mission

Empower teams to focus on high-value work by giving them a clear, connected operating system for their business.

  • Clarity: Single source of truth across modules.
  • Speed: Smooth UI with AJAX flows and quick exports.
  • Control: Tenant-level customization and audit logging by design.
  • Trust: Sensible permissions, approvals, and observability.
